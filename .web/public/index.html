    About GreenX

    Welcome to GreenX – a platform dedicated to mental well-being and support. Our mission is to provide a safe space where individuals can find resources, guidance, and AI-powered support for their mental health journey.

    🚀 Our Mission

    We believe that mental health matters just as much as physical health. GreenX is here to empower individuals, break mental health stigma, and offer instant emotional support when needed.

    What We Offer

    • 🤖 AI Chatbot – A caring AI for emotional support and wellbeing tips.
    • 📊 Sentiment Analysis – AI-based detection of emotions in conversations.
    • 🚨 Suicide Risk Assessment – Identifies potential signs of distress.
    • 📚 Resource Hub – Forums, crisis helplines, and self-help guides.
    • 📓 Journaling Feature – A digital diary for users to track emotions.

    💡 Why GreenX?

    Unlike generic chatbots, GreenX understands your emotions, offers real-time sentiment analysis, and connects you with the right support.
